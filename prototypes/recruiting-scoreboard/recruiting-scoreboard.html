Recruiting Scoreboard ▾

The Scoreboard tracks relationship progress across seven Key Recruiting Journey Steps for every school in your shortlist. The Quality Offer Score reflects how completely the relationship has been developed; the Athletic Fit Score (from the Grit Fit Engine) reflects how well your athletic profile matches that school's level of competition. Together they form the Offer Profile — Quality × Athletic Fit, sorted descending. Schools above the line clear the 35% Offer Profile threshold.

The Quality Offer Score is computed live from seven Key Recruiting Journey Steps (the offer-relevant subset of the 15-step Recruiting Journey schema): count of Yes ÷ 7 × 100%. The Athletic Fit Score is pulled from the GrittyOS Grit Fit Scoring Engine, mapped to each school's level of competition (Power 4: 13.9%, G6: 30.2%, FCS: 47.8%, D2: 67.6%, D3: 84.5% for this student). The journey-step booleans are read-only in the Scoreboard; they update only when the student marks a step complete on the school card itself in the Shortlist. Offer Profile = Quality Offer Score × Athletic Fit Score — both metrics required, neither substitutes for the other.
