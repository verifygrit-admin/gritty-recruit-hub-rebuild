Orange tokens = student needs to fill (Coach Last Name). Sand-colored tokens = auto-filled from profile, editable. Plain text = manually entered in the form.
On Copy or Email-to-Self: the message is logged to public.profiles.cmg_message_log (JSONB) with school name, scenario number, channel, recipient role, timestamp, and email-to-self flag. Phase 1 logging is fire-and-forget; no relationship-state inference is drawn from this log.
