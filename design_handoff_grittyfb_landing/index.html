What GrittyFB solves

Three problems your recruiting program has right now.

A Gameplan for Every Student

Every student-athlete's college fit is different. The cost of getting it wrong is generational. GrittyFB replaces guesswork with the same institutional-grade analysis used by families with the deepest cultural capital — athletic fit, academic percentile, true out-of-pocket cost, and long-term ROI for every program in NCAA Divisions I-FBS, I-FCS, II, and III.

No Balls Dropped

Coordinating communication across high school administrators, guidance counselors, coaches, families, college admissions officers, and college coaches is the work that breaks down. GrittyOS makes sure the right checkpoint happens at the right time, with the right information, every time.

The Platinum Opportunity

The conventional wisdom in recruiting points families toward a single Division I scholarship offer — statistically unavailable to most. GrittyFB surfaces the Platinum Opportunity: a selective institution, even at D-III or FCS, that produces stronger lifetime earnings, deeper alumni networks, and better long-term outcomes than waiting on a D-I offer to a less selective school. Why I built GrittyFB

A coach saved my college education. So I built the platform I wished I'd had — for the families who deserve it.

Playing big-time college football was my childhood dream.

But a week before my senior year of high school football began, I broke my foot.

Dreams became smoke. The future seemed completely uncertain.

But I had something most folks don't have, a legendary coach.

Not only was my high school coach a caring mentor to hundreds of student-athletes, but he also served as our athletic director and a guidance counselor. I have never known a better-connected coach nor someone who did more to change the lives of high school student-athletes.

Times have changed. High school athletics have too. The system has too many cracks, and far too many young athletes are falling through them.

I recognize the challenges facing my athletes. They know college football destinations but are unclear on the road that leads to them. Some have parents and coaches who can navigate the system. They land at selective schools that change lives. The ones without spend years searching for opportunities in places where they rarely materialize.

I built GrittyFB to close that gap.

The recruiting intelligence available only to the most connected coaches and counselors — fit modeling, financial analysis, process coordination, conference-by-conference timing — should be available to every student-athlete at every high school. Especially the ones the recruiting process overlooks.

I had someone who looked out for me. GrittyFB provides the same coverage in a new era.

That's the company. That's the mission. If your athletic department serves student-athletes who deserve better than the default path, I'd like to talk.

Chris Conroy

Founder, GrittyFB
